#### Quality Signals to Monitor

**âœ… Positive Signals** (You're doing well):
- They're asking questions (genuine curiosity)
- They're sharing specific details about challenges (opening up)
- They're connecting you to other people ("You should talk to...")
- They're proposing next steps ("Let's schedule...", "Send me...")
- Natural laughter or intellectual rapport building
- They're taking notes or referring you to others
- Time extends beyond planned (they're engaged enough to continue)

**âš ï¸ Warning Signals** (Adjust approach):
- Brief, polite responses without elaboration
- Looking around room, checking phone, distracted body language
- Generic responses ("That's interesting") without follow-up questions
- Vague deferrals ("Maybe reach out later" without timeline)
- Crossing arms, leaning back (closed off body language)

**Adjustment Strategies**:
- If too technical: Shift to business value and ROI
- If too business: Shift to technical credibility and architecture
- If too salesy: Shift to curious questions and learning mode
- If losing interest: Ask open question, listen more, find common ground
